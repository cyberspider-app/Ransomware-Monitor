# Support

Welcome to the support page for **Ransomware Monitor**.

If you're experiencing an issue or have a question, you've come to the right place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app collect any of my data?**
No. Ransomware Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for more details.

**The app isn't working as expected. What should I do?**
Try closing and reopening the app first. If the issue persists, please submit a report on our GitHub Issues page (see below).

**How do I report a bug?**
Head to our GitHub Issues page and open a new issue. Please include as much detail as possible, such as your device make and model and iOS or android version.

## Contact & Support

For bug reports, feature requests, or general questions, please open an issue on GitHub:

**[Submit an Issue](https://github.com/cyberspider-app/Ransomware-Monitor/issues)**

We'll do our best to respond as quickly as possible.

---

*Ransomware Monitor is an independent app. Support is provided on a best-effort basis.*
