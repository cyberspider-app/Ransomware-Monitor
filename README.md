# Ransomware-Monitor
Real-time ransomware victim intelligence and CISA KEV monitoring aggregated from multiple open-source feeds — free, no ads, no account, no data collection.

Ransomware Monitor is a free threat intelligence tool built for security professionals, CTI analysts, IT administrators, and researchers who need to stay current on ransomware activity and known exploited vulnerabilities without paying for a commercial feed.

The app aggregates victim reports from RansomLook.io, Ransomware.Live, and CTI.fyi — deduplicating and merging them into a single unified feed that refreshes automatically every 15 minutes. It also monitors the CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog for newly added entries, filtered to the most recent 90 days.

# RANSOMWARE FEED
Victim cards show the discovered date, targeted organisation, country, threat actor group, and which sources reported the incident. Expand any card for full source summaries and a one-tap copy button for use in reports, tickets, or briefings.

# CISA KEV MONITORING
Browse newly added entries from the CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog with full details: CVE ID, vendor, affected product, description, required action, due date, known ransomware campaign use, and associated CWEs.

# PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
Receive a local notification when new ransomware victims or new CISA KEVs are detected since the last refresh — whether the app is open or running in the background.

# ADVANCED SEARCH
Search across ransomware victims and CISA KEVs simultaneously. Filter by victim name, country, threat actor, CVE ID, vulnerability name, vendor, or product — with fuzzy or exact matching.

# WATCHLIST
Track specific victims, countries, threat actors, or keywords. Enable Watchlist View to filter the main feed down to only the entries that match your monitored terms.

# LEADERBOARD
Ranked views of the top five threat actors, countries, and reporting sources by activity over the past 24 hours or 7 days. Tied entries share a rank and display together.

# DATA SOURCES
• RansomLook.io
• Ransomware.Live
• CTI.fyi
• CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities Catalog (cisa.gov)

All sources are publicly available open-source intelligence feeds. No proprietary data, no vendor relationships, no paywalled content.

# PRIVACY
No account required. No user data collected. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs. The only data stored on-device is a small set of card identifiers used to prevent duplicate notifications — nothing leaves your device.

FREE
No ads. No in-app purchases. No subscription tiers. No data colected. Every feature is available to every user.
