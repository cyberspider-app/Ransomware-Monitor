# Partner With Us

**Ransomware Monitor** is an independent app focused on helping users stay informed about ransomware threats. We are open to partnerships with companies that want to contribute to that mission.

## How You Can Get Involved

### Threat Intelligence & Data
If your organization maintains threat intelligence feeds, ransomware tracking data, or security research that could improve the app, we'd love to hear from you. Contributions may include:

- Ransomware incident or campaign data
- Threat actor or group tracking feeds
- Indicators of compromise (IOCs)
- Vulnerability or exposure data related to ransomware

### Sponsorship & Partnerships
If your company is interested in sponsoring Ransomware Monitor or exploring a formal partnership, we are open to discussing arrangements that align with the app's mission of providing clear, unbiased security awareness to users.

## What We Offer in Return

- Attribution and acknowledgment within the app and repository
- Collaboration on features that benefit both parties
- Visibility with a security-conscious user base

## Get in Touch

To start a conversation, open an issue on GitHub with the subject "Partnership Inquiry" and we'll follow up from there.

**[Submit a Partnership Inquiry](https://github.com/cyberspider-app/Ransomware-Monitor/issues/new?title=Partnership+Inquiry%3A+&labels=partnership)**

Please include a brief description of your organization and what you're looking to offer or discuss.

---

*All partnership inquiries are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We are committed to keeping Ransomware Monitor independent, trustworthy, and user-focused.*
